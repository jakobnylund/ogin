The Pure Spirit of Swedish Lapland

In Boviken, just outside Skellefteå in Swedish Lapland, we craft spirits in harmony with nature. Beginning with the first shoots of spring and continuing through the changing seasons, we harvest fresh herbs, plants, and berries by hand—ensuring each gin is a reflection of the land.

Every bottle tells the story of a year in Boviken, capturing a unique moment in time.

Our Story

Born from the untouched beauty of Swedish Lapland

Our story begins with master distiller Jón Óskar, whose travels through Europe's distilleries sparked a profound inspiration. Along the way, he discovered something truly unique—the rare and remarkable Arctic Raspberry, found only in the wilds of our region.

Signature Cocktails

01

Arctic Raspberry Gimlet

ÓGIN Arctic
  • 5cl ÓGIN Arctic
  • 5–6 leaves of bog myrtle
  • 3cl lime juice
  • 2cl simple syrup

Garnish with arctic raspberries and bog myrtle. Serve over clear ice with cold neutral tonic water.

02

Cloud Sour

ÓGIN Cloud
  • 5cl ÓGIN Cloud
  • 2 tbsp cloudberry jam
  • 2cl honey
  • 3cl egg white
  • 3cl lemon juice
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters

Mix all ingredients in shaker for creamy head. Top with soda water and salt. Garnish with rosemary and cloudberries.

03

Nature & Tonic

ÓGIN Nature
  • 5cl ÓGIN Nature
  • 0.5cl lemon juice
  • Arctic raspberries
  • Pine shoots and bog myrtle

Serve over clear ice with cold neutral tonic water. Alternative: substitute with rosemary, regular raspberries, and extra lemon.

04

Barrel Martini

ÓGIN Barrel
  • 7cl ÓGIN Barrel
  • 1cl dry vermouth
  • Olives

Stir over clear ice carefully to limit dilution. Garnish with one or two olives.

05

Pink Gin Fizz

ÓGIN Pink
  • 3cl ÓGIN Pink
  • 2cl Aperol
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 2cl agave syrup
  • 3cl lemon juice
  • 3cl egg white
  • 10cl fruity sour beer
  • 6cl soda water

Garnish with lingonberry salt.
